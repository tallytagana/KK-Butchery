About KK Butchery

A legacy of quality that spans generations

Who Are We?

We are a traditional butchery based in the heart of New Brighton in Mbizweni Square, a central business district for the locals. KK Butchery is continuing a long tradition of your local corner butchery since 1978. We are pleased to be one of the independent retailers that make Mbizweni Square such an attractive and vibrant community.

As a local shop we can be responsive and attentive to our customers' specific needs in a way that is not possible for a large supermarket or chain. Our commitment to quality and personal service has made us a trusted name in the community for over four decades.

Our Vision

To be the leading traditional butchery in the Eastern Cape, known for our exceptional quality, personalized service, and commitment to our community.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with the finest quality meats through traditional butchery methods, expert knowledge, and personalized service that exceeds expectations.

Why Choose KK Butchery?

Expert Craftsmanship

With many years of butchery experience and skills we can offer our customers a full range of cuts and trims. We work with the whole animal so we can offer a wide selection.

Traditional Aging

Traditional butchers allow ageing and storing of meat before it is sold. KK Butchery uses ageing to improve the final flavour and tenderness of our meat.

Complete Transparency

You can see the product and watch it being prepared. Nothing is hidden and you can ask questions and get advice at the same time.

Quality Assurance

In recent years consumers have been worried by mystery meat scandals. If you want certainty about what's on your plate, we can tell you in detail.

Our Promise to You

High Standards

We pride ourselves on providing a personal, friendly and consistently high-quality service. We readily give advice on cuts and preparation.

Quality Service

For online orders, each is carefully prepared to your specification by an expert and hand-packaged, before being delivered to your doorstep.

South African Pride

At KK Butchery we like to keep it South African wherever possible. With your support, we've succeeded in maintaining the highest standards.

We Listen to You

Your feedback and needs drive our continuous improvement. We're always here to serve you better and exceed your expectations.
